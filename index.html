ABOUT ME

Full stack web developer with a bachelor’s degree in finance and professional experience in the energy industry. Earned a certificate in full stack web development from the University of Utah, where I acquired new skills in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Node.js, React.js and learned best practices for front and back end development. Known as an innovative problem solver in my profession, where I was able to create systems from scratch to automate tasks that would normally take days to complete. With each project, I worked on a team of three to four and enjoyed collaborating with others in developing mobile-first web applications. I’m looking forward to leveraging my web development skills and my finance background to become a part of a team that values quality-driven web applications.

Weather Forecast Appclose

Weather app that provides current and future weather forecasts in cities. This progam uses HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, and dynamic HTML. Inventory Management Appmore_vert Website Repository
Inventory Manaagement Appclose

Web app for a local business to streamline their steel yard’s inventory management. This application allows a worker to select the material they are either picking up or dropping of from a 60 acre lot. Once the item is selected they are directed on a Google Map with pins of where the item is located or should go. Once that task is performed the employee can send a auto populated email with what their task was and select a manager to send the email to. Utilized HTML, Bulma, CSS, and jQuery to create dynamic webpages that are easy to use and read. Used the Google Maps api as well as SMTPjs.com to email the inventory department.
